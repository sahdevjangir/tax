Frequently Asked
Questions

Who should file an ITR

An individual whose annual income is more then the basic exemption limit of
Rs2.5Lash should file an ITR. The basic exemption limit ofr senior citizen (60
years onwards and less then 80 years) is Rs 3Lakh,and for super senior citizen
is Rs 5lakh

How can i claim deductions for tax savings

You should file an income tax return to claim tax deductions for tax savings
such as ELSS,PFF,NSC investment and for payment such as housing loan
repaymets, insureance premium and donations.

I receive my salary income after deduction of TDS. Am I required
to file an income tax return?

you are required to file and income tax retun once your annual income exceeds
Rs 2.3Lakh.A diduction of TDS does not replace the requiremet to file ITR.
While e-filing your ITR, you should furnish the details of your annual income
claim deductions and credit for TDS deducted by your employer.

How do I check TDS details from my form 26AS?

You can check your Form 26AS from your e-filing login. ClearTax’s e-filing
software auto-populates the TDS details from your Form 26AS in your income
tax return.

How can i claim an icome tax refund

You can claim an income tax refund by e-filing your income tax return. An e-
filing enables you to claim credit for excess TDS paid and a tax refund.

What are the GST services offered by ClearTax?

ClearTax provides ClearTax GST filing software for accurate GST filing,
reconciliation and claiming ITC. ClearTax GST facilitates the generation of e-
invoices and e-way bill.

How to e-verify my ITR

You can e-verify your ITR withn 120 days form the date of filling. you can e-
verify using your net banking accon or Aadhar based OTP. a failure to e-
verify your ITR can invaldate your ITR filing
